**C-Eval** is a comprehensive evaluation suite specifically designed for assessing Chinese-language foundation models across multiple difficulty levels and disciplines. It covers 52 diverse disciplines ranging from middle school to professional examinations, providing a rigorous and standardized benchmark for the Chinese AI research community.

**Key features:**
- 13,948 multiple-choice questions across 52 disciplines
- Four difficulty levels: middle school, high school, college, and professional
- Covers STEM, humanities, social sciences, and other fields
- Bilingual explanations and answer keys for transparent evaluation
- Evaluation of 40+ prominent foundation models including GPT-4, ChatGPT, and Chinese-specific models

Published at **NeurIPS 2023**.
